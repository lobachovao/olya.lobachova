Case study

This case study highlights how direct user communication and campaign support helped identify common SaaS onboarding challenges and improve outreach quality.

Customer Problem

Many SaaS users struggled to launch effective outbound campaigns.

Common issues included:

  • 1Unclear messaging in cold outreach
  • 2Difficulty structuring campaigns
  • 3Slow onboarding and confusion during setup

As a result, some users delayed launching campaigns or requested frequent support.

My approach

I worked directly with users to identify pain points and improve their outreach process.

Key actions included:

  • 1Communicating with clients and collecting feedback
  • 2Creating scorecards to evaluate outreach message quality
  • 3Helping users structure campaigns
  • 4Experimenting with AI-assisted message generation
  • 5Supporting campaign setup in Instantly and HeyReach
Result

These efforts helped users launch campaigns faster and improve outreach quality.

Key outcomes:

  • 1Clearer messaging strategies for clients
  • 2Faster onboarding and campaign activation
  • 3Valuable user insights for product improvements.
